Discover Indore

In the heart of central India, Indore thrives as a city where rich history fuses with contemporary growth, offering an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

From the royal grandeur of Rajwada Palace to the modern vibrancy of Sarafa Bazaar, Indore offers a perfect blend of heritage and contemporary flair. Whether you're indulging in its famous street food, exploring its lively markets, immersing in its rich cultural traditions, or experiencing its evolving nightlife, Indore welcomes you to a city where the past and present unite, creating an unforgettable journey at every corner.

Must-Visit Places in Indore

Rajwada Palace

★★★★★
(1,234 reviews)

Rajwada Palace is one of Indore's most iconic and historical landmarks, reflecting the grandeur and royal heritage of the Holkar dynasty. This majestic palace is a perfect blend of Maratha, Mughal, and French architectural styles, showcasing the rich cultural history of Indore.

Lal Baag Palace

★★★★½
(2,142 reviews)

Lal Baag Palace is a historical palace located in Indore, built during the reign of the Holkar dynasty. The palace is known for its stunning blend of Maratha and Mughal architectural styles, showcasing a rich royal heritage. It was originally built as a royal residence for the Holkar rulers and is surrounded by lush gardens.

Jahaz Mahal

★★★★★
(1,653 reviews)

Jahaz Mahal, also known as the "Ship Palace," is a stunning historical monument located in Mandu, Madhya Pradesh. It is one of the most famous attractions in the region and is known for its unique architecture and picturesque setting.
