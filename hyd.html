Discover Hyderabad

In the heart of southern India, Hyderabad flourishes as a city where royal history blends with modern innovation, offering an unforgettable journey for every traveler

From the historic charm of Charminar to the modern tech hubs of HITEC City, Hyderabad offers a perfect fusion of tradition and innovation. Whether you’re savoring its mouth-watering cuisine, exploring its bustling markets, delving into its rich cultural heritage, or enjoying its growing nightlife, Hyderabad welcomes you to a city where the past and future come together, offering a unique experience at every turn

Must-Visit Places in Hyderabad

Ramoji Film City

★★★★★
(5,587 reviews)

Ramoji Film City is one of the largest film studio complexes in the world and a top modern tourism destination in Hyderabad. It offers an immersive experience into the world of filmmaking, with themed sets, live shows, and a variety of rides. Visitors can explore film sets, participate in studio tours, and enjoy attractions like the Star Wars-themed set, a mini amusement park, and an interactive science center. It's a must-visit for anyone interested in cinema, entertainment, or looking for a fun and unique experience in Hyderabad.

falaknuma palace

★★★★½
(1,156 reviews)

Falaknuma Palace is one of the most opulent and historically significant palaces in Hyderabad. Built in the 19th century, it served as the residence of the Nizam's guests and offers a glimpse into the grandeur of the Nizam’s era. Located on a hilltop, the palace offers stunning panoramic views of the city.

Buddha Statue on Hussain Sagar Lake

★★★★★
(3,000 reviews)

The Buddha Statue on Hussain Sagar Lake is one of the most iconic landmarks in Hyderabad and a symbol of the city's cultural heritage. Located in the middle of the lake, the statue stands as one of the tallest monolithic statues of Buddha in the world, measuring around 17 meters (56 feet) in height.
