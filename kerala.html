Discover Kerala

n the heart of South India, Kerala captivates with its blend of lush landscapes, tranquil backwaters, and rich cultural heritage, offering a journey that enchants every traveler.

From the serene backwaters of Alappuzha to the misty hills of Munnar, Kerala is a haven for nature lovers and cultural explorers alike. Whether you’re cruising in a houseboat, indulging in a Kathakali performance, or relaxing on its pristine beaches, Kerala invites you to unwind and embrace its natural beauty and spiritual charm.

Must-Visit Places in Kerala

Athirapally Waterfalls

★★★★★
(2,345 reviews)

Athirapally Waterfalls, often referred to as the "Niagara of India," is a majestic cascade nestled in the lush forests of Kerala. Surrounded by pristine landscapes, it offers a breathtaking view and a serene atmosphere, perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers

Wayanad

★★★★½
(5,890 reviews)

Wayanad, located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a serene paradise known for its lush forests, scenic waterfalls, and rich wildlife. Famous for attractions like Edakkal Caves, Soochipara Falls, and Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, it offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and tranquility.

Jatayu

★★★★★
(3,120 reviews)

Jatayu Earth Center, located in Chadayamangalam, Kerala, is a unique blend of adventure and mythology, centered around the legendary story of Jatayu from the Ramayana. The site features a massive sculpture of Jatayu, the mythical eagle, along with trekking trails, cable car rides, and breathtaking views from the top of the hill.
