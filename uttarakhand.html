Must-Visit Places in Uttarakhand

Valley of Flowers

★★★★½
(4,200 reviews)

The Valley of Flowers is a breathtaking UNESCO World Heritage Site in Uttarakhand, famous for its vibrant alpine flowers and picturesque views. It offers a serene trekking experience, perfect for nature enthusiasts like you who appreciate both beauty and adventure.

Auli

★★★★½
(4,200 reviews)

Auli is a popular hill station in Uttarakhand, known for its stunning snow-covered slopes and being one of India's best skiing destinations. It offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and various adventure activities like skiing and trekking.

Roopkund Trek

★★★★½
(4,200 reviews)

The Roopkund Trek in Uttarakhand is famous for the mysterious Roopkund Lake, known for its skeletal remains found in the lake's vicinity. It offers a challenging but rewarding journey through dense forests, alpine meadows, and snow-covered landscapes.
