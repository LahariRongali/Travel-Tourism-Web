Welcome to Kaziranga

Home to the majestic One-horned Rhinoceros and a UNESCO World Heritage Site nestled in the heart of Assam.

Where the mighty Brahmaputra flows, Kaziranga stands as a testament to successful conservation, where each sunrise brings new hope for its magnificent inhabitants.

Discover Kaziranga

Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is home to two-thirds of the world's Great One-horned Rhinoceros. This magnificent park spans across 430 square kilometers of pristine wilderness, creating a haven for diverse wildlife.

Beyond its famous rhinos, Kaziranga boasts a rich ecosystem with extensive elephant grass, marshland, and dense tropical moist broadleaf forests. The park hosts numerous other endangered species, including the Bengal Tiger, Asian Elephant, Wild Water Buffalo, and various species of migratory birds, making it one of India's most significant conservation success stories.

Must-Visit Places in Kaziranga

Kaziranga Orchid Park

★★★★★
(3,298 reviews)

Home to over 500 species of rare orchids, this beautiful park showcases the rich floral diversity of Northeast India. The park features a beautiful garden, a paper-making unit, and a restaurant serving traditional Assamese cuisine. Visitors can learn about orchid conservation and enjoy cultural performances in the amphitheater.

Kaziranga National Park Safari Zones

★★★★★
(2,892 reviews)

Explore four distinct safari zones - Central (Kaziranga), Western (Bagori), Eastern (Agaratoli), and Burapahar. Each zone offers unique wildlife viewing opportunities, with elephant safaris and jeep tours available. The Central and Eastern zones are particularly famous for rhino sightings, while the Western zone is known for its tiger populations.

Hoollongapar Gibbon Sanctuary

★★★★½
(2,789 reviews)

Located near Kaziranga, this unique sanctuary is home to India's only apes - the Hoolock Gibbons. The sanctuary also houses other primates like capped langurs, stump-tailed macaques, and northern slow loris. With its evergreen forest canopy, it offers excellent opportunities for wildlife photography and nature walks.
